Who are we?

We are a handful of friends who grew up with animals. They have become an integral part of our lives. For this reason, our goal is to give people a piece of this love in their everyday lives.

Why do we make this blog?

We want to give our readers useful knowledge about your favorite and hope we can improve their everyday life a little. In addition, we ourselves can still learn a lot from this blog.

Contact Us

Please use the Email if you have a question or if you need to contact us. We'll respond to your email as quickly as possible.

Animals are the bridge between us and the beauty of all that is natural. They show us what's missing in our lives, and how to love ourselves more completely and unconditionally. They connect us back to who we are, and to the purpose of why we're here.
